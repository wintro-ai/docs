Welcome to Wintro! Our platform empowers organizations to hire efficiently, cost-effectively, and with greater quality through employee referrals. We streamline the referral sourcing process for recruiters, while enabling employees to effortlessly provide valuable referrals and recommendations. The result? Organizations save money by filling roles more quickly, employees suggest colleagues they would love to work with and earn rewards, and candidates find jobs where they can thrive, develop, and advance within the company. Let us help you build stronger teams with Wintro.
